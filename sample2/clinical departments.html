    Emergency Medicine

    The Cardinal Santos Medical Center Emergency Room is under the auspices of the Department of Emergency Medicine.

    Led by top Philippine emergency medicine specialists, the Emergency Department Team of well-trained medical professionals is well equipped to provide emergent care to distressed patients, even those suffering from trauma and critical illness, any time of any day, 24/7. You can be sure of prompt and personal care and attention that any patient, young or old, critical or non-critical, deserves.

    For further details, please call (02) 727-0052 and we will be glad to assist you.

    Internal Medicine

    Cardinal Santos Medical Center is the preferred place of practice of some of the Philippines’ top practitioners in Internal Medicine. These specialists provide expert care for work-related conditions and injuries. They provide expertise in the prevention of such conditions to promote optimal health and safety in the workplace so you can come home at your best.

    The largest among the medical departments, the Department of Internal Medicine is composed of over 300 of the Philippines’ top specialists dealing with non-surgical diseases among adolescents and adults. It encompasses the following disciplines:

  • Allergy and Immunology
  • Cardiology
  • Dermatology
  • Endocrinology and Diabetology
  • Gastroenterology and Gastrointestinal Endoscopy
  • General Internal Medicine
  • Geriatric Medicine
  • Hematology
  • Infectious Diseases and Infection Control and Prevention
  • Medical Oncology
  • Nephrology and Hemodialysis
  • Neurology
  • Pulmonology
  • Psychiatry
  • Rheumatology
  • Toxicology and Pharmacology
