Cardinal Santos Medical Center

Cardinal Santos Medical center Speciality Center

The Cardinal Santos Medical Center community gathered at the third floor of the Philippine Center for Advanced Surgery building to formally open the hospital’s groundbreaking project, the CSMC Specialty Center. “We dedicate this center to every patient and client from whom we draw meaning into our profession. May the fervor of sense and service and the passion to truly care be upon each and every one of us,” said Atty. Pilar Nenuca P. Almira, CSMC president and CEO, in her closing remarks during the center’s grand opening last April 17.
