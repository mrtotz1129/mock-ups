Clinical Departments

Dental Medecine

The Department of Dental Medicine of Cardinal Santos Medical Center will definitely make you smile. It offers comprehensive dental services and is fully dedicated to the patient’s comfort and satisfaction. It offers:
  • General Dentistry
  • Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Endodontics (root canal therapy)
  • Prosthodontics (dentures and bridges)
  • Pediatric Dentistry
  • Periodontics (diseases of the gums and supporting tissues)
  • Oral implants and oral surgery
  • Orthodontics

    Internal Medicine

    Cardinal Santos Medical Center is the preferred place of practice of some of the Philippines’ top practitioners in Internal Medicine. These specialists provide expert care for work-related conditions and injuries. They provide expertise in the prevention of such conditions to promote optimal health and safety in the workplace so you can come home at your best. The largest among the medical departments, the Department of Internal Medicine is composed of over 300 of the Philippines’ top specialists dealing with non-surgical diseases among adolescents and adults. It encompasses the following disciplines:
  • Allergy and Immunology
  • Cardiology
  • Dermatology
  • Endocrinology and Diabetology
  • Gastroenterology and Gastrointestinal Endoscopy
  • General Internal Medicine
  • Geriatric Medicine
  • Hematology
  • Infectious Diseases and Infection Control and Prevention
  • Medical Oncology
  • Nephrology and Hemodialysis
  • Neurology
  • Pulmonology
  • Psychiatry
  • Rheumatology
  • Toxicology and Pharmacology

    • Women’s Care

    The Cardinal Santos Medical Center Women's Care is designed as a one-stop hub for various out-patient procedures and services especially catered to women. The unit provides women the necessary care needed to maintain good health and fitness.

    Endoscopy Unit

    The Endoscopy Unit of Cardinal Santos Medical Center is dedicated to the assessment and treatment of the interior surfaces of the gastrointestinal tract through endoscopy. Minimal invasive techniques through allows for shorter hospital stay and the absence of surgical scars.

    Eye Center

    The CSMC Eye Center offers expertise in multiple eye specialties for diagnosing and treating eye diseases.

    Hemodialysis

    The Hemodialysis specializes in caring for patients with kidney failure and those who need renal replacement therapy. The hemodialysis team aims to ensure utmost comfort and safety for its patients.

    Laboratory

    The Laboratory Department is equipped with up-to-date laboratory equipment for fast and accurate results.

    Pharmacy

    The Pharmacy Department is responsible in providing optimal pharmaceutical care to patients with 24-hour service.
    Radiology The Radiology Department offers a wide range of radiological procedures, using state-of-the-art imaging technology and performed by well-trained technologists, nurses and radiologists to ensure accuracy and safety of procedures.

    Rehabilitation Medicine

    The Department of Rehabilitation Medicine specializes in treating neck and low back pain, tendonitis and arthritis; work and sports-related injuries; patients recovering from stroke; brain and spinal cord injury; patients rehabilitating from joint replacement surgery, among others.

    Stone Center

    The Stone Center also offers specialized, minimally invasive urologic surgeries with minimal or no skin incisions.
